How to read this tool

The Thai Government Lottery draws twice a month — on the 1st and 16th of every month. This tool counts how often each number has appeared in each prize category within your chosen time window, so you can see which numbers are drawn most (or least) frequently.

Prize categories:

First6-digit jackpot — 1 number drawn per draw
Second6-digit — 5 numbers drawn
Third6-digit — 10 numbers drawn
Fourth6-digit — 50 numbers drawn
Fifth6-digit — 100 numbers drawn
Two DigitIndependently drawn 2-digit number (00–99)
Three FrontIndependently drawn 3-digit number — 2 drawn per draw
Three BackIndependently drawn 3-digit number — 2 drawn per draw
Near FirstFirst prize number ±1 — the two adjacent numbers

Frequency table — each row is a number, its total appearances in the window, and the draw dates it appeared (newest first).
Digit rank table (small, on the left) — for each digit position in the number, shows which digit (0–9) appears most often.
Recent draws grid (right) — individual winning numbers from the most recent draws in the selected window.

Default is most frequent at the top
