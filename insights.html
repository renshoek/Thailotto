How many times each number appeared in the selected window. Hover a bar for the exact count and percentage. Percentage = appearances ÷ total draws in window (numbers drawn multiple times per draw inflate this above 100% for high-volume prizes).

Statistical Candidates
Numbers that are most overdue relative to their own historical average gap — they've gone the longest past when they would normally reappear, based purely on their own track record in this dataset. A number with an avg gap of 10 draws that hasn't appeared in 25 draws is +150% overdue.
⚠️ Statistical observation only. The Thai lottery is independently drawn each time — past patterns do not guarantee future results. "Overdue" is a statistical measure, not a prediction. Each draw is an independent random event.
