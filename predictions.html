✗ What doesn't work
  • Score >24%: actual hit 13.8% — worse than random 19%.
  • Recency-only: worse than random at all window sizes.
  • Single exact prediction: top-1 hit 0.5% — 100 outcomes is a hard floor.
  • "Due number" logic: no confirmed mean-reversion.
  • Sample size: 458 draws can't confirm a 2pp edge (need ~2000).
  • High model certainty ≠ higher trust. Inverse holds in backtest.
