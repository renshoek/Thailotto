How Scout Scores Each Number
Signal 1 · Model P (60%)
Probability from the combined recency + base rate + overdue model (validated weights: 50/30/20, W=15). This is the strongest predictor — higher-ranked numbers get more weight.
Signal 2 · Number overdue (30%)
How far this specific number's current absence exceeds its own historical average gap. Only counted when the number has appeared ≥3 times — fewer appearances make the gap estimate too noisy. Reliability scales up to 8 appearances.
Signal 3 · Calibrated zone (10%)
Small bonus if at least one digit is in the 17–22% zone — the range where the backtest found a directionally positive hit rate (22.3% vs 19% baseline).
